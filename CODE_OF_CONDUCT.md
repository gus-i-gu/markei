# Community Conduct

Markei welcomes thoughtful technical, design, economic, privacy, and accessibility discussion.

Participants are expected to:

- engage with people respectfully and address ideas rather than identities;
- provide criticism that is specific, relevant, and actionable;
- avoid harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, or deliberate humiliation;
- protect private household information and remove personal data from examples;
- respect project scope, documented authority boundaries, and evidence requirements;
- avoid spam, manipulation, coordinated disruption, and undisclosed conflicts of interest.

Project spaces may be moderated to preserve safety, relevance, and productive collaboration. Content may be edited, hidden, or removed, and participation may be restricted when behavior materially violates these expectations.

Concerns should be reported privately to the repository owner when possible. Reports should describe the behavior, location, and relevant context without unnecessary redistribution of harmful or private material.
